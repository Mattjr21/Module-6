Personal Life

Beyond my professional work, I'm an avid outdoor enthusiast who enjoys hiking and landscape photography. On weekends, you'll likely find me exploring new trails or experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen.

I'm passionate about continuous learning and often spend my free time taking online courses in subjects ranging from philosophy to new programming languages.

Music plays a significant role in my life as well. I've been playing guitar for over 10 years and occasionally perform at local open mic nights.

Cooking

Music

Playing guitar and attending live concerts keeps me connected to the emotional power of sound and rhythm.

Reading

From science fiction to philosophy, books expand my thinking and inspire new ideas in both work and life.
